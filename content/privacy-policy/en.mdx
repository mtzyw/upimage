# Privacy Policy

> Last updated: August 8, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to ImgEnhancer.ai (hereinafter referred to as "we," "ImgEnhancer," or "ImgEnhancer.ai"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy aims to clearly explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our AI image enhancement services. By using our services, website, or products, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use our AI image enhancement services, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
- **Payment Information**: Billing details processed through our secure payment provider (Stripe)
- **Images**: Photos and images you upload for enhancement processing
- **Communication**: Messages you send to our support team

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our services, including enhancement settings and processing history
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Log Information**: Server logs, error reports, and performance data
- **Cookies**: Small data files stored on your device to improve your experience

### 3. Analytics and Performance Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our AI models and service quality:
- Enhancement success rates and processing times
- Popular enhancement settings and scale factors
- Service performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Service Provision

We use your information to:
- Process and enhance your images using our AI technology
- Manage your account and subscription
- Process payments and billing
- Provide customer support

### 2. Service Improvement

- Improve our AI enhancement algorithms
- Optimize processing speeds and quality
- Develop new features and enhancements
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

### 3. Communication

- Send service-related notifications

- Notify you of important updates or changes to our services

## How We Handle Your Images

### 1. Image Processing

- Images are processed using our secure AI infrastructure
- Processing is performed on secure servers with encryption
- Images are temporarily stored during processing (typically a few minutes to hours)
- Enhanced images are made available for download for a limited time

### 2. Image Storage and Retention

- **Temporary Storage**: Images are temporarily stored during processing and for download
- **Automatic Deletion**: Images are automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days
- **No Long-term Storage**: We do not permanently store your personal images
- **Secure Transfer**: All image uploads and downloads use encrypted HTTPS connections

### 3. Image Privacy

- We do not view, share, or use your images for any purpose other than enhancement
- Images are processed by automated AI systems without human review
- We do not train our AI models on your personal images without explicit consent
